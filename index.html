Good questions

Frequently asked

Is Verity free?

Yes. The free tier gives you all the tracking, trends, your doctor report, and Ask Vera's curated, clinician-reviewed answers. Verity Premium adds Ask Vera AI — live, grounded conversations — as a subscription. The core app stays free.

Is Vera a doctor?

No. Vera is a companion that shares general, clinician-reviewed information — not medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.

Where is my data stored?

On your device by default — you can use Verity with no account at all. If you sync, it's stored encrypted on our own servers, hosted in the EU. Wherever you live, you get the same strong, GDPR-level protection. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Does Verity send my data to an AI?

Only if you choose Premium. The free Ask Vera answers from a clinician-reviewed library right on your device — no AI, nothing sent. With Verity Premium, Ask Vera AI sends the question you ask — with your consent and without identifying details — to an AI provider, which answers grounded in Verity's vetted women's-health library. We never use your health data to train any AI.

Do I need an account to use it?

No. Verity works offline in local-only mode. An account is optional and only needed if you want to sync across devices or back up your data.

When does it launch?

We're in the final stages of building. Join the waitlist and you'll be among the first to know when Verity is available on the App Store and Google Play.

Who is Verity for?

Women worldwide, aged 18 and over, at any hormonal life stage — with a special focus on perimenopause, menopause and postmenopause, including women on or considering HRT, and the people who support them.

Verity is a personal health-tracking companion, not a medical device. Information provided by Verity and Vera does not constitute medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
